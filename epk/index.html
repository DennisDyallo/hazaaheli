Biographies

Haza Aheli

Short Bio

Haza Aheli is a visionary French dancer and choreographer based in Toulouse, known for blending ethnic traditions with contemporary movement. A professional dancer since 2007 with over 17 years of teaching experience, she is the artistic director of the Hazayana Collective. Her work is a "sacred movement" that transcends performance. From the dust of Burning Man to the stages of Boom Festival, she invites audiences into a mystic world of embodied emotion, intricate musicality, and inventive force.

Full Artist Biography

Haza Aheli explores the powerful intersection of ethnic heritage and contemporary expression. Originally trained in graphic arts and photography, she discovered oriental dance in 2007 and quickly gravitated toward Tribal Fusion and Sacred Dance, training with the American pioneers of the movement. She co-founded the duo Nadyka, performing together for seven years before forging her own artistic path. Haza weaves influences from hip hop, tutting, waving, and contemporary dance into her Tribal Fusion foundation, creating choreographies that are as technically precise as they are spiritually resonant.

Her artistic philosophy focuses on "embodiment over appearance," using dance as a therapeutic and mystic tool to purify emotion and manifest beauty. A dedicated community builder, she created the Ethnik Season Festival (seven editions) and the Fusion Flow Festival (since 2023). This approach has taken her around the world, teaching and performing in Japan, Bali, Europe, and Costa Rica.

Haza is the founder of Ahelia Retreats, an immersive artistic wellness project, and the director of the Hazayana Collective—known on the French stage circuit as SORA Dance Company (formerly Marabyliane, est. 2012). Her impressive performance resume includes major international stages such as Burning Man (USA), Envision Festival (Costa Rica), Boom Festival (Portugal), Tribal Bliss, and Sparkles Festival (Geneva).

The Hazayana Collective

Group Bio

Under the artistic direction of Haza Aheli, The Hazayana Collective—performing locally in France as SORA Dance Company—represents the cutting edge of Fusion Bellydance. This multi-disciplinary troupe embodies the "Fusion Illusion" style, fusing the mystic and solemn world of Tribal Bellydance with the percussive energy of hip hop, tutting, waving, and contemporary urban arts.

The company comprises Haza (artistic director & choreographer), Marie Castellano and Sarah Debaud (dancers & choreographers), and musician Stew Bamrah (live didgeridoo & sound therapy). Their repertoire includes signature productions such as RITUEL (a mystic, dreamlike 1-hour show), ABYSSE (a luminous spectacle), and ENVOL (featuring aerial fabrics, veils, and flag wings).

The collective offers an immersive visual and emotional experience, known for intricate musicality—often collaborating with electronic artists like CloZee, Moonkin, and Tipper—and the ability to transform a festival stage into a temple of sacred movement. Beyond stage shows, the company offers roaming performances, parade choreography, artist residencies, and video/clip collaborations.

Performance Offerings

Flexible booking packages from single performances to full-weekend residencies.

1. The Stage Experience (15 – 50 Minutes)

A modular, high-production show designed for main stages or intimate theaters.

  • The Vibe: A theatrical journey blending Fusion Bellydance, storytelling, and "Sacred Bass" music.
  • Visuals: Elaborate custom costuming and programmed LED light integration for high-impact nighttime slots.
  • Live Elements: The show transcends backing tracks by incorporating live musicians, including didgeridoo and percussion, adding an organic, primal layer to the electronic beats.
  • Fire Option: For outdoor nighttime slots, the troupe collaborates with elite fire partners to integrate pyrotechnic arts into the choreography.

2. The "Embodied Fusion" Workshop (60–90 Minutes)

Proven success: 50+ attendees at Boom Festival.

An accessible yet deep workshop suitable for all skill levels. Haza leads participants through yoga-infused warm-ups, isolation techniques, and fusion choreography. It is more than a dance class; it is a community connection experience perfect for festival mornings or afternoon activation slots.

3. Ecstatic Dance Hybrid Set

A full journey for the dancefloor. This is not just a DJ set, but a live performance hybrid.

  • Format: DJ set accompanied by live vocals, didgeridoo, and live instrumentation.
  • Atmosphere: Guiding the crowd from meditative lows to high-energy peaks, creating a safe container for free expression and movement.

Technical Requirements

Lighting Design

Lighting is a crucial element of our storytelling. We prefer to collaborate directly with the venue's lighting technician/designer to program cues that properly frame the dancers and highlight the performance. We provide mood boards or cue sheets in advance.

Audio System

Professional-grade PA with high-fidelity sub-bass response required. Monitors required for dancers on stage. We provide our audio mix in MP3 and WAV formats in advance and bring it on USB on the day.

Stage Space

Clear, flat performance area with a minimum of 7m width x 7m depth. If the space is smaller, please communicate this in advance so we can adapt.

Rehearsal & Backstage

A sound and lighting rehearsal on stage is required in the hours before the performance. A dedicated dressing room (loges) is needed to prepare and securely store costumes and accessories.
